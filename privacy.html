Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026

Fund of Regret is a public on-chain portfolio and editorial project. This page explains what data may be collected when you visit fundofregret.com and how it is used.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand basic traffic patterns — pages visited, approximate geography, device type, and session duration. This helps the Fund understand how far regret travels.

Google Analytics is only loaded if you consent. If you decline, no analytics scripts run and no data is sent to Google. Your choice is stored in your browser (localStorage) and respected on all future visits.

If you previously accepted and want to withdraw consent, use the Cookie Settings link in the footer.

What is collected

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Approximate country and device type
  • Referral source (how you arrived)
  • Anonymous session identifiers assigned by Google

No names, email addresses, wallet addresses, or financial information are collected through analytics. No data is sold or shared with third parties beyond Google Analytics' standard processing.

On-chain data

The portfolio positions displayed on this site are verified on public blockchains. Wallet addresses are public by design — this is the point. No on-chain data is private or protected.

Cookies

This site stores one value in your browser's localStorage to remember your analytics consent preference (for_consent). No tracking cookies are set before consent. If you consent to analytics, Google Analytics may set its own cookies as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-party links

This site links to external services including Etherscan, Solscan, Mintscan, Dropstab, X / Twitter, and Telegram. These services have their own privacy policies. Fund of Regret has no control over their data practices.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed via @fundofregret on X or Telegram.

This is not a legal entity. Fund of Regret is an editorial and media project. This policy reflects good faith transparency, not regulatory compliance theater.
