Constitution

Manifesto

Fund of Regret is a public on-chain portfolio of beaten altcoins, turned into a series. Not a fund in the legal sense. A media format, a character, and a public bet — all at once.
The Fund buys only what:
  1. The market has already punished severely
  2. Still has a clear mechanism or infrastructure logic
  3. Causes fatigue, irritation, or ridicule in the crowd
  4. Can be defended with a thesis — not just hope
  5. Can be held publicly without hiding from the drawdown
The Fund never buys:
  1. Pure hope without a mechanism
  2. An asset just because it "dropped a lot"
  3. An idea that can't be explained in 2-3 sentences
  4. What can't be shown on-chain
  5. What you'd be ashamed to enter publicly
The Fund is obligated to:
  1. Show losses as eagerly as profits
  2. Acknowledge where the thesis may break
  3. Separate irony from self-deception
  4. Speak briefly
  5. Stay in character
Hope is not a thesis.
The position is public. So is the mistake.
Not financial advice. Financial regret.
